Introduction

This website is operated by MelexTech, an independent educational technology website and online platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit melextech.dev (the "Website").

By using this Website, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use the Website.

Information We Collect

The only personal information we collect is what you voluntarily provide through our Contact page:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Message content

We do not require accounts, login systems, passwords, or user profiles. We do not collect comments or additional personal information beyond what is listed above.

About Our Tools

Our SaaS tools (unit converters, password strength checker, regex learning tools, and other educational browser tools) run entirely in your browser. They do not transmit your input to any server.

How Information Is Used

We use the information collected through the Contact page solely to respond to your inquiries. We do not use your information for marketing, newsletters, or any other purpose without your explicit consent.
